Every blog starts with a single post that nobody reads. This is mine.

There's something quietly freeing about that — writing into the void, without the weight of an audience. Just you and the blank page and the strange act of turning thoughts into sentences.

## Why write at all?

Writing is thinking made visible. The moment I try to explain something, I discover how well I actually understand it — or don't. Gaps appear. Assumptions surface. Half-formed ideas either solidify or dissolve.

So I write to think, not to perform.

## What this space is

This blog has no particular theme. It's a place for:

- Things I'm figuring out
- Ideas I keep returning to
- Observations that feel worth keeping

If something here is useful to you, that's a happy accident. The primary audience is future-me, looking back.
